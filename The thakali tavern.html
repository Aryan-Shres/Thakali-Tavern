Our Story

Authentic Thakali Cuisine Since 2010

Founded by Chef Rajesh Thakali, our restaurant brings the authentic flavors of the Thak Khola region of Nepal to your table. With recipes passed down through generations, we pride ourselves on preserving traditional cooking methods while incorporating modern presentation.

Our ingredients are sourced locally whenever possible, and we work directly with Nepalese farmers to import specialty spices and grains that give our dishes their distinctive flavors.

In 2020, we were honored to receive the "Best Nepalese Restaurant" award from the International Culinary Association, a testament to our commitment to quality and authenticity.
